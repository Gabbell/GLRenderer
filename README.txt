All features are implementend along with the extra credit.

The 1,2,3 and 4 keys switch between the different states.

1: Original parsing of the height map
2: Skipping points according to the user-defined skip size
3: First pass of Catmull Rom
4: Second pass of Catmull Rom

P: Switch to points rendering
T: Switch to triangle rendering

Right click mouse: Switch to camera free mode
W,A,S and D: Move around
